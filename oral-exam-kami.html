Topic 1 · Artificial Intelligence

AI 对学习的影响

Impact on Learning

A
Student A
Have you noticed that almost everyone is using AI tools like ChatGPT for their assignments lately? What's your take on this?
B
Student B
Well, I think it's a double-edged sword. On one hand, it's super efficient for gathering information. But on the other hand, it might make students lazy.
A
Student A
Exactly! If we rely on AI too much, we might lose our ability to think critically.
B
Student B
True. So I guess the key is to use it as an assistant, not a replacement for our own brains.

Topic 2 · Aerospace

探索太空的意义

Significance of Space Exploration

A
Student A
China has made amazing progress in space exploration recently, like the moon landing missions. Do you think it's worth the huge cost?
B
Student B
Definitely! It's not just about planting a flag; it's about pushing the boundaries of human knowledge and developing new technologies.
A
Student A
I agree, but some people say we should spend that money on solving problems on Earth first, like poverty or climate change.
B
Student B
I see your point. However, space tech often helps us solve Earth's problems too, like satellite communication and weather forecasting.

Topic 3 · Traditional Culture

年轻人与传统文化的结合

Youth Meets Tradition

A
Student A
Nowadays, many young people are getting interested in "Hanfu" or traditional crafts. Do you think traditional culture is making a comeback?
B
Student B
For sure! Social media plays a big role. Short videos make traditional culture look "cool" and modern.
A
Student A
That's a good point. But do you think we should keep the traditions exactly as they were, or should we change them to fit modern life?
B
Student B
I believe we should modernize them. If traditions don't evolve, they might be forgotten by the next generation.

Tips · Exam Day

考试小贴士

三个让表现更自然的实用技巧

💬
不要背稿子
老师最忌讳听起来像在背书。对话中多加点自然过渡语
"Well..." "You know..."
"That's a good point"
👀
眼神交流
看着搭档的眼睛，而不是盯着地板或天花板，体现沟通意图
Eye Contact
= Communication
🤔
万能补救
如果突然卡壳，微笑着用这些短语缓冲，比直接停下来好得多
"Wait, how should I put this..."
"Let me rephrase that..."
